Million Challenge

Account deletion instructions

You can permanently delete your Million Challenge account and its associated game data in two supported ways.

Delete from inside the app

  1. Open Million Challenge.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. If your account uses email and password, enter your current password to confirm.
  5. Confirm the deletion request.

Delete by email request

  • Send your request to IhbaliQusai@gmail.com.
  • Use the subject line: Delete My Account.
  • Include the email linked to the account or your in-game name to help us locate the account.

Data deletion removes your account record, public profile, matchmaking entries, invitations, and related game progress stored for that account. Some local device data may also be cleared during the in-app deletion flow.

Privacy policy: https://sites.google.com/view/privacypolicymillion

Request partial data deletion

You can also request deletion of specific data — such as your match history or friends list — without deleting your account. To do so, send an email to IhbaliQusai@gmail.com with the subject line Partial Data Deletion and specify which data you would like removed. We will process your request within 30 days.
